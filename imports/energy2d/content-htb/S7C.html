Energy Detective 7C: Discover a vertical temperature gradient

  • Table of Contents

This model shows the effect of natural convection in a house.

  1. Run the model, turn the graph on, and observe the temperatures.
  2. Record the steady temperatures of the three thermometers, T1, T2, and T3, when the ceiling is present.
  3. Click the ceiling (the green piece) and press the DELETE key on your keyboard to remove it.
  4. Record the temperatures of the three thermometers again.

You may turn the streamlines on to observe the convective flow in more details.

After you have completed your investigation, answer the questions in your workbook.
